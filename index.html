Legacy

A Century of Agricultural Excellence.

Pampanga State Agricultural University (PSAU) is a 500-hectare institution in Magalang, Pampanga, dedicated to "HUMANE" development. Founded in 1885, it offers 25 accredited programs to 7,000+ students and is globally recognized for agricultural innovations like its patented sweet tamarind. Through advanced research and international partnerships, PSAU remains a leader in sustainable agriculture and community development.

About the Platform

Map-Alibut is a bespoke spatial intelligence tool engineered for the PSAU community, bridging the gap between physical campus growth and digital accessibility.

Utilizing high-precision geospatial data, the platform provides a low-friction experience for students to locate facilities.
